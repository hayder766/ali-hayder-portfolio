About Me

I am Ali Hayder, a final-year Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) student at Green University of Bangladesh, with a strong specialization and growing professional focus on Software Quality Assurance (SQA). I am passionate about ensuring the reliability, performance, usability, and overall quality of software systems throughout the entire development lifecycle. My academic background has equipped me with a solid foundation in software testing methodologies, quality standards, system analysis, and defect management, enabling me to identify issues early and contribute to building stable, scalable, and high-performing applications.

Through continuous learning and hands-on practice, I have developed strong analytical, problem-solving, and attention-to-detail skills, essential for ensuring software quality in both manual and automated testing environments. I am committed to applying best practices in SQA, collaborating effectively with development teams, and maintaining a user-centric approach to quality assurance. My career goal is to leverage my technical knowledge and practical experience to contribute to the development of reliable, secure, and high-performance software solutions that address real-world challenges and meet global quality standards.
